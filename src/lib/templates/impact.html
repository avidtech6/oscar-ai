1.0 Introduction

This Arboricultural Impact Assessment (AIA) has been prepared in accordance with British Standard 5837:2012 "Trees in relation to design, demolition and construction". The assessment evaluates the potential impacts of the proposed development on the existing trees within and adjacent to the site.

1.1 Purpose

The purpose of this assessment is to:

  • Identify trees that may be affected by the proposed development
  • Assess the significance of potential impacts
  • Provide recommendations for tree protection and mitigation measures
  • Inform the design process to minimize arboricultural constraints

5.0 Conclusions

The proposed development will have varying levels of impact on the existing tree population. With appropriate mitigation measures and careful implementation of the recommendations in this report, the impacts can be minimized to acceptable levels.

The key recommendations are:

  1. Implement Tree Protection Zones (TPZs) around all retained trees
  2. Employ an arboricultural method statement for construction near trees
  3. Regular monitoring by a qualified arboriculturist during construction
  4. Replace removed trees with suitable species at appropriate locations
